ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

What Is AI?

Artificial Intelligence or AI,
is a wide branch of computer science concerned with buildig smart machines
that can perform tasks which typically require Human Intelligence.
AI and its advancements in Machine Learning & Deep Learning
are creating a paradigm shift in every sector of the Tech Industry.


How AI is used?

AI has 2 broad categories; Narrow AI & Artificial General Intelligence(AGI).
Narrow AI: It is the kind of AI that operates a specified scope
and is a simulation of Human intelligence.
Its focused on performing a single task extremely well.
This is powered by Machine Learning & Deep Learning.
AGI: It is a machine with general intelligence
& much like a human being, where it can solve any problem.


Places where AI is used.

  • Smart Assistants (Siri & Alexa)

  • Disease mapping

  • Song & TV recommendations on Netflix & Youtube

  • Conversation Bots

  • Robo Advisors
