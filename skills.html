ECMA Scripts

European Computer Manufacturers Association or ECMA,
is a standard script language,
influenced by programming languages such as Self, Perl,Python & Java.
JavaScript,Jscript & ActionScript are governed by this specification.
ECMA script is used for client-side scripting.
